About Me

Hi there!

I'm Ellen, an 18 year old artist currently studying computer science. I love drawing, sculpting and experimenting with all kinds of different mediums. A lot of my work takes inspiration from nature, especially cute animals... I've found that I often gravitate towards creating miniaturized versions of objects and creatures into collectibles. I hope you enjoy checking out my artwork and thanks for stopping by!
